Why Have Patios Cleaned?

Patios are exposed to constant weather, foot traffic, dirt, algae and moss throughout the year. Over time this build-up can leave surfaces looking dull, slippery and unsafe. Professional pressure washing removes stubborn grime and restores the original appearance of your patio.

Regular patio cleaning also helps prevent long-term damage caused by moss, weeds and trapped moisture. Keeping your patio clean improves the appearance of your garden space and creates a safer area for family and visitors.

At Pacys we use professional pressure washing equipment designed to clean patios safely and effectively without causing unnecessary damage to the surface.

Whether your patio is stone, concrete, block paving or slabs, we can restore it back to a fresh and tidy condition.

Benefits Of A Clean Patio

A professionally cleaned patio instantly improves the appearance of your outdoor area. Removing black spots, algae and dirt can completely transform old and tired surfaces.

Clean patios are also much safer. Moss and algae become extremely slippery in wet weather and can create a serious slipping hazard if left untreated.

Protecting And Sealing Your Patio

After cleaning, sealing your patio can help protect the surface from future staining, weed growth and water penetration.

A quality patio sealer enhances the appearance of the slabs while helping maintain the clean finish for longer periods.

Patio sealing can also reduce future maintenance and protect surfaces against weather damage throughout the year.

The Right Equipment

Using the correct pressure washing equipment is essential when cleaning patios properly. Too much pressure can damage surfaces while poor equipment may fail to remove deep dirt and algae.

We use professional surface cleaners, commercial pressure washers and specialist treatments to achieve outstanding cleaning results.

Our equipment allows us to clean efficiently while protecting your patio surface and surrounding areas.
