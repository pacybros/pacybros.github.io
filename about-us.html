About Us.

Meet Patrick & Frazer

Pacys External Cleaning Services is a family-run business based in Doncaster, founded by two brothers — Patrick and Frazer Pacy — with a passion for hard work, honest service, and helping properties look their very best.
Our journey started small with a local wheelie bin cleaning round known as The Bin Brothers. What began as a simple service quickly grew through word of mouth and customer recommendations across South Yorkshire.
As we built strong relationships with local homeowners, more and more customers began asking if we could also clean driveways, patios, roofs, gutters, and exterior walls. Seeing the demand for reliable exterior cleaning services, we decided to take things further.
We invested in professional training, specialist equipment, and industry knowledge to expand our services and officially launch Pacys External Cleaning Services.
Today, we proudly provide professional exterior cleaning throughout Doncaster and surrounding areas, helping homes and businesses restore and maintain their appearance with safe, effective cleaning methods and a friendly, reliable approach.
